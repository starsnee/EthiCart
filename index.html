Why EthiCart?

EthiCart is a no strings attatched, easy and simple way to create more sustainability in your purchases without putting the effort of research onto you. EthiCart makes sustainability more accessable so anyone can contribute to helping keep the environment safer, and by supporting local produce, to move the work to better labor practices and livable wages.
